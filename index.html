Monitor smart-contract risk before it becomes an exploit.

Sentinel is a prototype for a Blockchain Security Engineer. It lets a reviewer connect a wallet, inspect a target contract, score operational risk, record findings, and publish a signed audit attestation with a mock on-chain proof trail.

Why this prototype fits the role

1
Risk-first workflow.
Security engineers usually need triage before deeper manual review, not just raw scanner output.
2
On-chain accountability.
A signed attestation is more role-aligned than a generic dashboard because it mirrors review approval, handoff, and audit evidence.
3
Response readiness.
The pause-drill view makes the app useful during incidents, not only during development.
Prototype behavior is local and mocked for speed, but the product structure is ready to extend with ethers.js, wallet providers, contract metadata, and audit evidence storage.
